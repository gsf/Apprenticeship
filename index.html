The P'unk AvenueApprenticeship

What is it?

The P'unk Avenue apprenticeship program is a six-month learning experience at a purpose-driven company for a specifically motivated designer or developer.

The program consists in a cohort of two to three apprentices who learn together through both a cannon of formative reading—industry standards text as well as refelctive books about purpose-driven work—and authentic client projects to build technical skills.

  • Some skills in design or development
  • Interested in urbanism, knowledge creation, wellness, arts and culture, and purpose driven companies
  • Wants to work on authentic projects with real responsiblities
